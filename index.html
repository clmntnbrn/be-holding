Believe Eve is a boutique PR consultancy where people and personality reign supreme. Our team of senior consultants have worked in industries spanning fashion, interiors, retail, beauty, health, food and drink.

 

We’ve worked at agencies big and small, and had roles in-house working for corporate giants.

We’ve incorporated all of the best bits (and none of the bad) into the service we offer today, and have a great record of providing our clients with intelligent communication that makes a difference.

 

We love our clients and we’ll do whatever we can to spread that feeling amongst journalists, stylists and consumers alike. Contagious enthusiasm is a result we continuously strive for.
